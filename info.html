Hello, I am a designer, technologist, crafter, and educator living in Brooklyn, NY.

My work focuses on the intersection of craft and emerging technology through wearables and soft circuitry. I am passionate about designing and developing the future of tactile interfaces while preserving traditional techniques.

I graduated with my master’s degree from Interactive Telecommunications Program at NYU in Spring of 2016. My thesis project, Cyberknitics — a wearable that generates music from the rhythm of knitting — seeks to answer the question, "will cyborgs knit?". I am currently a Design Engineer at Teknikio where I design electronics kits for kids and teach soft-circuitry workshops.
