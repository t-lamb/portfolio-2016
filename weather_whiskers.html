Weather Whiskers

A cat-themed weather app for iPhone & Android

I was the lead designer for this project while working at Mindspark, an IAC company. I worked on a small team to establish the user experience, interface design, brand identity, and artwork for the iOS and Android apps. More at weatherwhiskers.com.

User Experience —

Our goal was to make the experience “so easy, a cat could use it”. Our users would need to be able to add and switch between cities, check the forecast, and share their weather on social media. We also strove to provide accurate weather while delighting the audience with an app seemingly for cats and by cats.

Content Creation —

For each weather condition we created custom icons, art, and copy in the voice of lolcats – internet cat memes.
